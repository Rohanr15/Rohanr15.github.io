Electric Vehicle Projects

Selected projects related to hybrid systems, battery design and vehicle modelling.

Hybrid Vehicle Modelling and Analysis

Objective: Optimise total energy consumption (fuel + battery) for a power-split hybrid vehicle architecture.

Procedure: Used the ELVIO Simulink toolbox to simulate drive cycles and implemented a start-stop control strategy. Engine starts were minimised based on battery SOC and speed thresholds.

Results: Battery energy consumption decreased by 48%, fuel consumption dropped by 2.1%, and engine starts reduced from 61 to 9.

Battery Pack Specification for a BEV

Objective: Design a battery pack for a 300-mile range BEV that could also deliver 150 kW peak power for at least 20 seconds.

Procedure: Compared two cell types using real experimental data. Performed calculations for capacity, power output, and total weight.

Results: Selected Cell A for lower pack weight (53.8 kg lighter) while still meeting all performance criteria. Gained deep insight into EV powertrain design trade-offs.
